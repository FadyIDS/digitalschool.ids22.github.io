School Inventory Management

The school Warehouses and Stores management is one of the important lists that exist within the Digital School Management System.

    School stores

    • The store list facilitates the management of warehouses and school warehouses and maintain the school inventory and follow up such as school uniforms for students, books and school tools of varieties, types, companies, suppliers, registration of sales and procurement invoices, registration of any damage or damage within the school, as well as follow-up reports on the school stores.

    Features and role of Digital School Management System in School Inventory Management

    The list of stores in the Digital School System helps you organize and manage school stores such as stores of school uniforms, books, tools, etc

  1. Register all stores in the school.

  2. Register items and types in each store.

  3. Registration of companies and suppliers.

  4. Possibility to make inventory of stores.

  5. Possibility to make an inventory of invoices.

  6. Make purchase invoices for items and record it on the supplier's account.

  7. Make sales invoices for customers.

  8. Permission to hand over tools, books or school uniforms to each student and employee.

  9. The possibility to follow the movement of every item.

  10. Possibility of quantity adjustment.

  11. The possibility of transfers from store to store.

  12. Register and add units in each store.

  13. Recording depreciations.

    14. Store reports

    • Item movement report.

    • Quantity adjustment report.

    • Report store transfers.

    • Depreciation report.

    • Report an item delivery authorization.
