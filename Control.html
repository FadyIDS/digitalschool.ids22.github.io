    Management of control works and examinations

  1. Registration of an exam absence

    • A page to record students' absences from the exam by article and date.

  2. Add control

    • If there are more than one control in the school, the name of the control and its educational stages will be added.

  3. Add committees

    • You can add committees for each control individually and determine the number of students in the committee and the beginning of the seating numbers, after adding the committee, you can adjust the number of students in it and the numbers of sitting in the absence of students.

  4. Add seating numbers

    • Here students are added to the committees added before, the class is determined and shows the number of students who are not added seating numbers and choose the method of distribution to them (boys thus girls - girls thus boys - mixed).

  5. Adjusting seating numbers

    • You can delete or modify a pre-added seating number as soon as the number changes it will be adjusted.

  6. Add secret numbers

    • Sets of secret numbers are created and seating numbers are determined, and the beginning of the secret numbers, the beginning of the seating numbers, the number of students will be determined and then the distribution begins.

  7. Grading settings

    • Each grade and its beginning and end pecentage will be determined.

  8. Adjust grades for the reviewer

    • The grades are adjusted to the reviewer by displaying the grade statement for the reviewer and modifying and keeping.

    The control member work

  1. Control members

    • Control members are registered and their role in the control is determined, taking into account the name of the control and the name of the department.

  2. Registration of committee observers

    • It specifies the name of the subject, the test date, its duration, the name of the observer and whether it is a primary observer or an alternative.

  3. Recording a manual presence and departure of the control

    • Hours and attendance of the employees are recorded in the control and you can search for a particular month's attendance through the page.

  4. Protocol for the opening and closing of the control

    • A report that records the time of the control opening and closing with the head of the school control and then members, the date, time, members present and the printing of the report are selected.

  5. Approval of the completion of the curriculum

    • The name of the control, the head of the control, the date, the name of the material and the observer are selected and then the declaration is printed.

  6. Control business statistics

    • Through it, what is recorded during the day divided by control, section and class of (exam - correction - review - monitoring and final review).

  7. Question-and-answer paper movement

    • The name of the giver and receiver of the question and answer paper is recorded after the date, control, section, and article have been specified.

  8. Committee monitoring statements

    • A report showing the names of the main observers and reserves according to the pre-recorded by choosing the date and time of the start of the test.

  9. Connect users to classes

    • The department, specialization, employee name, educational stage name, class, department name, article and chapter name are selected.

    Monitoring grades and results

  1. The results of the first and second half streamlining

    • Monitoring grades in the edu step up school management program we choose the stage, class and then export the excel file to be added grades and then uploaded it again to the system.

  2. Add the results of the second round

  3. Monthly results are monitoring

  4. Monitoring the articles results

  5. Determined the articles grades

    • The grades of the materials are monitored manually by selecting the class and the material and then showing the students and starting to insert the grades and memorize.
